# Cashio

**CASHIO AI** is a smart affiliate cashback platform that allows users to shop through partner links and receive a share of the commission as cashback.

Cashio works within major affiliate ecosystems including Amazon, Shein, and Booking, making online shopping more rewarding with AI-powered tracking and distribution.

---

## Overview

Cashio simplifies affiliate rewards by automatically sharing commissions with users who shop through our links.

Instead of traditional affiliate models where only publishers earn commissions, Cashio redistributes a portion of earnings back to users.

Our system uses AI-powered tracking and automation to ensure accurate cashback calculation and distribution.

---

- AI-powered affiliate tracking
